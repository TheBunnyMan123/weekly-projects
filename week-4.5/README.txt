TimeCalc - Time Unit Conversion for the TI-84 Plus CE/CSE

Units:
- seconds
- minutes (60 seconds)
- hours (60 minutes)
- days (24 hours)
- weeks (7 days)
- months (~30.4 days)
- years (365.2425 days)

Controls:
- up arrow: scroll up
- down arrow: scroll down
- enter: change value to calculate against
- clear: exit
